Welcome

Hello!! Welcome, feel free to read on to know what's this site for.

So what's this for?

The purpose is simple. This site provides a platform where registered citizens can post requests to the municipal corporation to collect garbage in their neighborhood.


How to register?

Just contact the authority. Contact details are given in the Contact Us page. You will be briefed about the documents required for registration and the process of registration. Once successfully registered, you will get login credentials, using which you can login and post a request for garbage collection whenever you find garbage piles around your neighborhood or any other place.

How to post a request?

Follow these simple steps:

  • Login with your credentials and click on 'New request'.
  • Fill the form by entering a brief description, upload an image of the garbage pile and choose the location in the given map.
  • Click submit and your request will be sent to the authorities.
  • You can check the status of your request on 'Your requests' page.

For any other queries you can contact us through the details given on the Contact Us page.
